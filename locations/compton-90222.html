90222 leak questions

We are selling an older 90222 home. What plumbing documentation helps?

Three items move escrow: a current lateral camera report, a supply pressure test with materials noted, and records of any major repairs with permits. We produce all three in one visit, formatted for a disclosure file.

Is original galvanized an automatic fail for insurance?

Policies vary, but documented condition beats assumptions in every case. A pressure history and materials report gives your broker something concrete, and where replacement is due, a quote in the file shows the path.

How do we prioritize when everything in the house is old?

By evidence, ranked. Active failures first, then systems showing progression, then the merely elderly on a watch list with their tells named. You get that ranking in writing, and nothing gets bundled into urgency it has not earned.
